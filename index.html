How to be heard
It’s safe to say there are more writers now than at any other time in history.Someone get a gun and shoot me. That’s a lot of competition. Seriously though, why do we write? Why are all of us pursuing writing in the face of the increasingly limited attention spans of the broader public? It’s not like we’re making much money at it, if any. We write to express ourselves, the reason for existence of this blog.
